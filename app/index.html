OUR EQUIPMENT

We have newer Volvo trucks (2020-2024) with a big comfortable cabin, inverter, and a fridge. Every truck has a dashcam and a tablet for E-log. Pre-pass, EZ pass, and fuel cards are provided to the driver. Orozco Trucking provides dry vans and reefers that are regularly maintained.

DRY VAN

Dry Van is the preferred type of trailer to deliver goods. Products such as clothing, furniture, and electronics are commonly shipped via dry van trailers, though they also can be used to move machinery and parts, nonperishable foods, and even household goods.

REEFER

A refrigerated container is often used to deliver temperature-sensitive and perishable cargo, such as fruits, vegetables, meat, etc. Running a reefer is a bigger responsibility.

WE HAUL:
